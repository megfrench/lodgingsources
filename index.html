Weekend Opportunities

What is the YRRP?

The Yellow Ribbon Reintegration Program (YRRP) is a Department of Defense-wide effort to promote the well-being of National Guard and Reserve members, their families, and communities, by connecting them with resources throughout the deployment cycle.

To accomplish this, the DoD hosts hundreds of weekend-long events across the country every year. These events require significant logistical support, including massive hotel room blocks, large-scale conference center general sessions, dozens of breakout rooms, and extensive catering services to feed hundreds of attendees.

Why should hospitality professionals care?

Weekend Occupancy Boosts

YRRP events typically occur over weekends (Friday through Sunday), filling massive blocks of rooms exactly when corporate travel occupancy normally drops off.

Massive F&B Minimums

These events require feeding hundreds of soldiers and their families simultaneously, bringing in substantial, guaranteed Food & Beverage revenue for your property.

Guaranteed Master Billing

No chasing down hundreds of individual credit cards. Lodging Sources handles the Master Bill, ensuring your property gets paid reliably and on time.

VA Medical &
Self-Care Housing

Beyond weekend events, the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) regularly issues Self-Care Housing Contracts. When a Veteran travels to a major VA hospital for outpatient care, daily treatments, or pre-surgery preparation, the government pays to accommodate them in local hotels.

These Veterans do not require nursing care or hospital beds—they simply need a safe, comfortable place to sleep close to their treatment facility. Lodging Sources acts as the logistical bridge between the VA's social work teams and local hotels, managing the bookings and the billing.

Why hotels love VA Medical Contracts:

Consistent Weekday Occupancy

Unlike weekend events, medical lodging primarily occurs during the week (Monday - Thursday), perfectly complementing event-based revenue models to keep your hotel full 7 days a week.

Multi-Year IDIQ Contracts

These are often Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contracts spanning 3 to 5 years, providing incredibly stable, long-term revenue predictability for your property.

Supporting Our Veterans

It offers your staff the deeply rewarding opportunity to provide exceptional hospitality and comfort to those who served, without taking on any medical liability.
