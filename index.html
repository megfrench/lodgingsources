What is the YRRP?

The Yellow Ribbon Reintegration Program (YRRP) is a Department of Defense-wide effort to promote the well-being of National Guard and Reserve members, their families, and communities, by connecting them with resources throughout the deployment cycle.

To accomplish this, the DoD hosts hundreds of weekend-long events across the country every year. These events require significant logistical support, including massive hotel room blocks, large-scale conference center general sessions, dozens of breakout rooms, and extensive catering services to feed hundreds of attendees.

Why should hospitality professionals care?

Weekend Occupancy Boosts

YRRP events typically occur over weekends (Friday through Sunday), filling massive blocks of rooms exactly when corporate travel occupancy normally drops off.

Massive F&B Minimums

These events require feeding hundreds of soldiers and their families simultaneously, bringing in substantial, guaranteed Food & Beverage revenue for your property.
