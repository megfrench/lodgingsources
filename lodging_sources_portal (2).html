Core Capabilities for Government Buyers

We eliminate logistical friction. One contract, one master invoice, zero hassle for your agency.

YRRP Specialization

Extensive knowledge of Yellow Ribbon Reintegration Program SOWs. We source General Session rooms, Breakouts, Parlor rooms, and ADA compliant lodging.

GSA Compliance

All lodging accommodations and catering services are negotiated and sourced to strictly adhere to current GSA Per Diem rates for the localized ZIP code.

Master Billing

We manage the Master Bill with the hotel and catering vendors. The government receives a single, unified Firm-Fixed-Price invoice via WAWF.

On-Site Coordination

We provide full on-site physical representation during the event to manage logistics, A/V checks, catering flow, and act as the primary liaison for military cadre.
